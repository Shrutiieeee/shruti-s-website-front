Meet the Artist

Hi! I'm Shruti — a passionate artist who breathes life into colors and sketches. My world revolves around creativity, emotion, and storytelling through art. Whether it's a graphite pencil sketch, vibrant colored pencil work, or an expressive acrylic painting, each piece holds a story close to my heart.

My Creative Journey

From childhood doodles to commissioned masterpieces, my journey has been a blend of passion, learning, and soulful expression. I specialize in:

  • Acrylic Paintings on canvas
  • Colored Pencil Sketches
  • Graphite Portraits
  • Crafting
  • Wall Paintings

Every artwork I create is an extension of emotion and devotion — made with love, for people who truly appreciate the beauty in detail.
